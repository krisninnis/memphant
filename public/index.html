How it works

From zero to full context in seconds

A simple loop that keeps every AI you use perfectly in sync with your project.

1

Store your project

Fill in your project name, what it's about, your goals, key decisions, and next steps. Scan a local folder to auto-discover files and your tech stack.

2

Copy for your AI

Pick your platform — ChatGPT, Claude, Grok, Perplexity, or Gemini — and click copy. Memephant shapes your context perfectly for that AI's style.

3

Paste it in and work

Paste at the start of any chat. The AI gets up to speed instantly and starts helping — no re-explaining needed.

4

Paste the response back

When the AI makes decisions or updates your plan, paste its response back into Memephant. It detects any changes and shows you a diff before applying anything.

5

Your memory is always current

One click applies the update. Your project stays in sync — ready for the next AI, the next session, the next idea.

Features

Everything your project memory needs

Built around one rule: a non-technical user should understand what to do within 5 seconds.

🔄

Five platform formatters

Each AI gets a tailored handoff — not a generic dump. ChatGPT, Claude, Grok, Perplexity, and Gemini each have their own optimised format.

🔍

Paste-detect-diff-apply

Paste any AI response and Memephant automatically detects project updates, shows you exactly what will change, and applies it in one click.

⚡

Smart export mode

Large projects auto-condense to fit inside each AI's context window. Never hit a limit mid-session.

PRO
📁

Folder scanning

Link any local folder to auto-discover important files, your tech stack, and README summaries. Keeps your memory grounded in reality.

🔒

Secrets redaction

API keys, tokens, and credentials are automatically stripped before every export. Your secrets stay on your machine, always.

📋

Project templates

Start fast with five pre-built templates: SaaS, Freelance, Writing, Research, and Job Search. Your context is structured from day one.

☁️

Optional cloud backup

Sign in to sync your projects across all your devices. Fully encrypted in transit. 100% opt-in — core features work completely offline.

🔁

Auto-updater

New releases install with one click, directly from inside the app. No manual downloading, no fuss.

🌍

Works everywhere

Native desktop app for Windows, macOS, and Linux. Browser extension for quick captures. Mobile PWA for on-the-go access.

🏠

Local first

Your data lives on your machine by default. No account required to use any core feature.

🚫

No analytics

No telemetry. No trackers. Your data stays local by default. Cloud Backup is optional if you sign in, and Local AI is optional on your device.

Secrets stay secret
