# Memephant: the bet

**Decision (23 April 2026):** Memephant is my serious six-week bet.

For the next six weeks, when Memephant work conflicts with LeadClaw
or BrightFoundry, Memephant wins unless there is a genuine production
fire (paying customer blocked, money moving incorrectly, legal exposure).

LeadClaw stays in maintenance mode — respond to signups, fix breakages,
no new features. BrightFoundry client work continues but I do not take
on new clients until the six weeks are up.

Why: the folder-watcher feature is the only thing in any of my three
products that could plausibly become a defensible, paid, multi-user
product. LeadClaw and BrightFoundry are revenue today; Memephant could
be revenue tomorrow at a larger scale. Worth the focused bet.

Review date: 4 June 2026. If by then Memephant has not produced either
(a) 50+ engaged free users or (b) a clear signal this is a product
people pay for, I revert to treating it as a side project.
